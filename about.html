About the Dog Food Dispenser

Our dog food dispenser was designed with convenience and simplicity in mind. With automatic dispensing and adjustable portion sizes, it's never been easier to keep your dog fed and healthy.

Our easy-to-clean design makes it a breeze to maintain, and with its durable construction, it's built to last.

How it works

To use the dog food dispenser, simply fill the hopper with dry dog food and set the portion size using the control panel. The dispenser will automatically dispense the correct amount of food at the designated feeding times.
